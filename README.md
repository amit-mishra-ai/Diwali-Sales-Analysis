## Overview
This project presents an **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** of Diwali sales data to identify customer purchasing patterns and generate actionable business insights.  
The analysis focuses on understanding **customer demographics, buying behavior, and sales trends** to support data-driven decision-making during festive seasons.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior  
- Identify high-value customer segments  
- Evaluate product category performance  
- Generate insights to support targeted marketing and sales optimization  

---

## Dataset Description
The dataset includes transactional and demographic information collected during the Diwali sales period.

**Key Attributes:**
- Gender  
- Age Group  
- Marital Status  
- State  
- Occupation  
- Product Category  
- Purchase Amount
